Understanding your stats #

  • Level & XP increase with activity.
  • Daily Streak counts consecutive days with at least one message.
  • Voice Activity % reflects time actively participating (unmuted).
  • Emoji Stats include reactions and emojis used in messages.

Privacy & access #

Tips for power users #

Troubleshooting #

  • Stats not loading? Try Refresh; new users may have minimal data.
  • Missing recent activity? Refresh to fetch latest records.
  • Emoji section empty? Use emojis more and check back later.
