What it is #

  • A private thread/channel where you and staff can talk 1:1.
  • Used for issues better handled away from public chat.
  • Only you and authorized staff can view the tickets you open.

How it works #

  1. Go to the Support channel and click the Support button.
  2. A private ticket channel is created automatically.
  3. Describe the issue clearly and attach any evidence.
  4. Staff will claim your ticket and follow up with next steps.

If you canâ€™t see the button, check if channel embeds and interactions are enabled on your client.

Ticket types #

Server issues

  • Role or permission problems
  • Channel access or visibility issues
  • Report of rule violations or incidents

Bot or command problems

  • Commands failing, errors, or unexpected behavior
  • Permission or cooldown concerns
  • Feature requests or feedback

Appeals & sensitive concerns

  • Appeals for mutes/bans/timeouts
  • Harassment, safety, or privacy concerns
  • Other matters requiring discretion

What to include #

  • Short summary of the issue (one sentence).
  • What happened, where, and when (include timezone).
  • Links to relevant messages (copy link) and usernames/IDs if needed.
  • Screenshots or a short clip; blur sensitive info if necessary.
  • What you tried already (if anything).
