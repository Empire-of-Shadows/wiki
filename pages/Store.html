/store — Shop for Items in Empire of Shadows

What it does: Opens the in-server store where you can browse and buy items using your embers (our server’s currency).

How to Use

  1. Type /store in any text channel.
  2. A dropdown menu will appear where you can choose a category or view all items.
  3. Select a category to see what's available.
  4. Use the Next and Previous buttons to scroll through pages.
  5. Click the green Buy button next to an item to purchase it.

Buying Items

  • You must have enough embers (use /userstats to check).
  • When you buy an item, your embers are deducted and the item is added to your inventory.
  • Some items have limited stock, once they’re sold out, they’re gone.
  • If something goes wrong during the purchase, your embers will be refunded.

What You’ll See

Each item shows the following:

  • Name, the name of the item
  • Price, how many embers it costs
  • Stock, how many are left or “Unlimited”
  • Description, what the item is or does

Important Notes

  • Only you can interact with your store menu, others can't click your buttons.
  • All purchased items go to your personal inventory.
  • You can use store items later, such as power-ups or unlockables.

Tips

  • Check the store regularly, new items may be added!
  • Save embers for limited or special items.
  • Have an idea for something to add? Suggest it in #suggestions.
