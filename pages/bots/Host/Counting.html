Counting Game — User Guide

TL;DR

  • Numbers only; digits 0–9; max 12 digits; no edits.
  • Cooldown ≈2s; no double-posting unless idle/grace windows apply.
  • Mistakes are removed; bot posts expected number; milestone roles every 100.
  • Top counter holds “Master Counter.” View ranks via /leaderboard counting.

Where to play & goal #

  • Only post numbers in the designated counting channel(s). Check the channel topic if unsure.
  • Count upwards together, one message at a time: 1, 2, 3, 4, …

Basic rules #

  • Post exactly the next number—no text, no emojis.
  • Use only digits (0–9), no spaces or symbols.
  • Number length limit: up to 12 digits.

Cooldowns & double-posting #

  • You can send a valid number once every ~2 seconds.
  • No two numbers in a row unless:
    • ≈15 seconds have passed since the last valid number, or
    • The channel has been idle ≈30 seconds, or
    • A short “streak protection” window is active right after a mistake and you post the correct next number.
  • The “Master Counter” has a stricter double-post timer (≈3× longer).

Mistakes, penalties, and edits #

  • Wrong number or rule break:
    • Your message is deleted.
    • You may get a temporary “out-of-order” role.
    • The bot posts the current expected number to reset flow.
  • Brief “streak protection” may apply immediately after a mistake.
  • Editing a counting message is disallowed; edited messages are removed.

Milestones & celebrations #

  • Every 100 numbers, the poster may receive a milestone role.
  • The bot may react to valid numbers and post celebratory notes.

Master Counter role #

  • The top contributor on the leaderboard is automatically assigned the “Master Counter” role.
  • If the leader changes, the role is moved accordingly.

Leaderboard command #

Use this slash command to view the counting leaderboard.

Command

  • /leaderboard counting

What it shows

  • A ranked list of users with their total correct counts.
  • User mentions with scores; pagination if >10 results.

Options

  • top — how many users to display (1–50). Default: 10.
  • include_pending — include freshest cached increments (default: true). Set false for fully saved data.

Rate limits and timing

  • Light per-server cooldown (~1 use/3s) with cached results for performance.
  • If cache is warming or unavailable, you may see a short notice—try again shortly.

Possible responses

  • A leaderboard embed (single page or with pagination controls).
  • “The counting leaderboard is empty.” if no data yet.
  • A short error message if something goes wrong.

Quick FAQ #

  • Can I add text with my number? No. Post the number only.
  • Are edited messages counted? No. Edits are disallowed and may be deleted.
  • Why couldn’t I post two numbers in a row? Double-posts are only allowed after a delay or during idle periods; otherwise wait.
  • My leaderboard score looks slightly behind—why? Updates may be batched. include_pending shows cached totals; otherwise you see persisted data.
  • Why did I get an “out-of-order” role? You posted out of sequence or broke a rule; it’s temporary when you resume counting correctly.

Questions or issues? Ask a moderator. Have fun counting!
