Empire of Shadows — Server Guidelines

Last updated: 2025-01-01 • These rules keep the server friendly, safe, and fun.

TL;DR

  • Be respectful. No harassment, hate, slurs, or personal attacks.
  • No ads, invites, or DMs promoting anything.
  • No spam, impersonation, or drama-baiting.
  • Keep it legal and safe for work.
  • Use music/game bots fairly. Don’t hijack queues or troll games.
  • Report issues via #support → ticket. Don’t escalate publicly.

Community Rules #

  • No Partnerships or Promotions: No unsolicited ads, server invites, or DM promotions.
  • Respect Others: No hate speech, harassment, slurs, or bullying. Banter is fine—respect boundaries and de‑escalate.
  • No Spam or Excessive Pings: Don’t flood channels, chain messages, copypastas, or impersonation.
  • Keep It Legal: No piracy, cracked software, or illegal content. Follow Discord’s Terms ↗.
  • Appropriate Content: No NSFW/NSFL. Avoid graphic, shocking, or inflammatory content.
  • No Disruptive Behavior: Don’t bait, troll, stir drama, or be toxic. Be helpful and chill.
  • Names Must Be Mentionable: If we can’t tag you, a bot may assign a new server nickname and DM you both names.
  • No Age Talk: Don’t include age in usernames or public chats.
Examples (tap to expand)
  • Spam: rapid-fire messages, mass reactions, or repeated pings.
  • Promotion: “Join my server” DMs or link drops without staff approval.
  • Disruption: derailing topics, baiting arguments, targeted sarcasm.

Music Bot Guidelines #

  • Respect Song Choices: Don’t skip just because you dislike a track—be fair.
  • No Queue Hijacking: Don’t bury others’ picks or clear queues without consent.
  • Volume & Effects: Use boosts and effects responsibly.
  • Don’t AFK Hog: If you’re not listening, make room for others.
  • Let Everyone Vibe: Music bots are for fun, not control. Toxicity may lead to mutes/bans.
Good etiquette
  • Use text channel commands unless voice controls are enabled.
  • Ask before skipping community picks.
  • Keep volume reasonable for shared spaces.

Game Bot Guidelines #

  • Play Fair: No trolling, sabotaging, or griefing.
  • Respect Turn Order: Be ready on your turn; repeated timeouts may be skipped.
  • No Command Spam: Don’t spam start/cancel or repeat commands.
  • Don’t Hijack Games: Respect private lobbies; ask before joining public ones.
  • Use the Right Channels: Keep game commands in game channels.
  • Be Cool: Win or lose, keep it friendly. No flaming or gloating.

Enforcement & Appeals #

  • Progressive moderation: Reminder → Warning → Mute/Timeout → Kick/Ban (severity may escalate faster for serious violations).
  • Context matters: Staff may consider intent, history, and impact.
  • Appeals: If you believe a decision was unfair, open a ticket in #support. Keep it respectful; staff will review privately.

How to Report #

  1. Copy the message link (or take a screenshot if needed).
  2. Go to the #support channel.
  3. Open a ticket and paste the link with a short note.

Staff will handle reports privately and fairly. Please avoid public callouts.
