üì¨ Submit a suggestion #

Use the slash commands below to share your idea. You can keep it public or submit anonymously.

/suggest submit

  • suggestion_text (required): your idea, up to 2000 characters
  • anonymous (optional): True/False
  • category (optional): Bot Feature, Server Improvement, Event Idea, Rule Change, Other
/suggest submit suggestion_text:"Add a community game night" anonymous:false category:"Event Idea"

/suggest template

Pick a structured template to make your idea clearer:

  • Bot Feature ‚Ä¢ Server Rule ‚Ä¢ Event Proposal ‚Ä¢ Channel Request

Note: A short cooldown applies to prevent spam.

üîé Browse, search, and mine #

/suggest search

  • query (optional): text to match
  • category (optional): filter by category
  • status (optional): Pending, Under Review, Approved, Implemented, Rejected, On Hold
  • author (optional): filter by author
/suggest search query:"music" category:"Bot Feature" status:"Pending"

/suggest mine

See your submissions privately, including status, category, short ID, preview, and votes.

‚ùì FAQ #

Where are suggestions posted?

In the suggestions channel with a dedicated discussion thread.

Can I edit or delete my suggestion?

If you need a change after posting, add a follow-up comment or submit a revised suggestion.

How do I track my ideas?

Use /suggest mine to view your submissions and their current status.

Is anonymous really anonymous?

Your name is not shown publicly. Avoid including personally identifying information in the text if you want to remain anonymous.
