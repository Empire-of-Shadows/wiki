Info to include #

  • Exact command or action attempted, including options/arguments.
  • Links to messages (right-click copy link) or the channel name.
  • Error messages and screenshots; small clips help a lot.
  • Frequency: happened once, intermittent, or every time.
  • Other users affected: confirm if others can reproduce.

Severity levels #

Critical

  • Widespread outages, crashes, or data loss risk. Immediate attention required.

High

  • Major feature broken for many users; common commands unusable.

Medium

  • Feature partially broken or annoying regression; workarounds exist.

Low

  • Cosmetic issues, typos, minor inconsistencies, or rare edge cases.
