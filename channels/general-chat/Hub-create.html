Overview #

Temporary Voice Channels are auto-created when you join the Voice Hub. You instantly become the channel owner and can control access, name, and visibility. Channels are removed automatically when inactive.

How ownership works
  • Joining the Voice Hub spawns your personal VC and grants you ownership.
  • If you leave, ownership may transfer or be claimable after a short time.
  • Moderators can override settings when needed.

Permissions #

  • Owners: Full control of their own temporary VC via voice commands.
  • Moderators: Can use voice commands in any temporary VC.
  • Admins: Can manage, clean, and override settings globally.
